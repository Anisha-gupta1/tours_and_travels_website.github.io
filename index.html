places to visit in

north bengal

hills

North Bengal has a worldwide fame for the beautiful hills. These hills includes places like: Darjeeling, Mirik, Kurseong, Kalimpong,etc. Darjeeling is also known as "The Queen of Hills" and is famous for its Aromatic tea. It is also known for the panaromic view of the "Kanchenjunga". Also suitable for trekking, the weather out here is soul-soothing.

dooars

The Dooars is a large region and is dotted with many towns and cities. The largest cities in the region stretching from the Darjeeling foothills to the Arunachal Pradesh foothills are Siliguri and Jalpaiguri. Cooch Behar, Alipurduar, Dhupguri, Malbazar, Mainaguri, Falakata and Birpara are the major cities of the Dooars in West Bengal.

sikkim

Sikkim is a beautiful land of manifold tribes and races of people living together. This state is begifted with scenic beauty. The mountains, rivers, waterfalls,etc. add a never-ending charm here. This stae also has India's first 'Skywalk'. Must enjoy the beauty of places like Gangtok, Lachung, Gurudongmar Lake, Rhenock, Ravangla,etc.

bhutan

Discover the "Land of the Thunder Dragon". People out here have their own language, also called as,Dzongkha. Famous places include: Paro, Thimpu, Paro Taktsang monastary (Tiger's Nest), and many wildlife sanctuaries.

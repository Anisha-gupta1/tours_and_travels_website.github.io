our services

car rental services

Paradise Travels brings forth the most reliable car rental services for the clients. Travel in the car is always enjoyable as it gives a sense of freedom to the traveller, but one cannot carry his car everywhere on the tour. Therefore, we are here for you. We offer all kinds of cars on rent for organising tours. SUVs like Mahindra Bolero(AC), Mahindra Scorpio etc. and small cars as Maruti Van(non AC) Tata Indica(AC) etc.

Water rafting

The most adventure-filled sport, Water Rafting, people are fond of this too. We have well-educated and well-equipped guides with us for this event. It is possible and available only for Dooars and Sikkim travel as it is carried out in River Teesta. Guides provide you the essentials and also help you enjoy this adventure. So, go on and enjoy it.

jungle safari

Another adventure with us for you is, The Jungle Safari. This service is available only for Dooars travel. Our guides go along with our guests to ensure their safety. Safaris include Tiger safari, Elephant safari, and many more.. Also a visit to the National Parks and sanctuaries.

book a hotel for vacation

We also provide hotel booking as well as resort booking services. Places like Darjeeling and Sikkim are filled with natural beauty. You can book a resort or homestay to enjoy the scenic beauty and natural flora and fauna.
